About Me

Growing up in the Western foothills of Maine, I was a bright and ambitious kid, with an eye for tech and a passion for music. Initially, I chased that musical ambition. I ended up getting into an elite music school, and I hated it. It was the most demoralizing, soul-sucking experience of my life. Luckily, around the time I was wondering if I could escape the hell I'd found myself in, I met a woman, and found in her the greatest partner I could ever ask for. She encouraged me to do what I do best: take the leap no matter how stupid it may seem. She invited me to move to Canada, and I was delighted at the oppurtunity to start a new life. I spent ages looking for my new career path, one that fit my wants and needs for a job and also could keep us safe, and as I looked more into web development I realized it was pretty much everything I needed. Now, I'm taking the leap, learning every day, and growing as a dev.
